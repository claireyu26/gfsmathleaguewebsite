FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Math League?

The Math League is an organization that promotes mathematical problem-solving through competitions and resources for students of all levels.

Who can participate?

Students from elementary to high school can participate. Some competitions may have specific age or grade-level requirements.

How do I register for a competition?

You can register by visiting our Registration Page and following the instructions provided.

Is there a registration fee?

Some competitions may require a small registration fee. Please check the specific event details on our website.

How can I prepare for the competitions?

We offer various study resources, past problems, and online materials to help participants prepare.

Where can I find the competition results?

Results will be published on our Results Page after the competition.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

If you are interested in volunteering, please visit our Contact Page and reach out to us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote accessibility in mathematics and nurture mathematical talent. There is no entry cost to the competitions. We hope to empower confidence and passion in mathematics through our original high-quality problems and the incredible prizes supported by Wolfram, LIVE by Po-Shen Loh, and Jane Street.
